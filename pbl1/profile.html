John Stephens

Hi there! I am currently the Fashion and Style Writer at Vogue in New York City. I am First Nations (Ojibwe) and grew up on the Nipissing First Nation reserve in Ontario, Canada. After earning my Bachelor of Journalism degree from Ryerson University in 2014, I moved to New York to begin my career as a fashion editor at Footwear News, a role I held for three years. I have also freelanced for Vogue, Elle, Mr. Porter, Hazlitt, Refinery 29 & more.
